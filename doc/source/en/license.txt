=== Zarafa Subscription
In this module you can extend the numbers of users with Outlook access to Zarafa. Three users have access via Outlook without any subscription key. If you want more users to have access you may buy a subscription pack. 
The subscription key has two components: a base key and an additional user key.
Please insert the key into the specific (base or user) field an click the button _"key upload"_.

At the tab  _"Installed Subscriptions"_ all licences are shown. You will get more information at the tab _"Licenced Log"_.
